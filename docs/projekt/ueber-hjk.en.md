# About HJK

71 years old. Sleep apnea patient since 2009. Software developer — not by profession, but by curiosity and necessity.

I live in Baden-Württemberg, Germany, and started systematically analyzing my CPAP therapy in 2024. What began as a spreadsheet has become a standalone analysis system with over 3,000 commits, a PostgreSQL database, automated night reports, and a Raspberry Pi that autonomously collects data every night.

I work with AI — first ChatGPT, since September 2025 Claude. Not as a gimmick, but as a development partner. Four system generations have emerged this way, each better than the last.

## Why pseudonymous?

I write here as "HJK", not under my full name. Not out of secrecy, but pragmatism: as a privately insured patient in Germany, I'd rather a Google search for my name not lead directly to detailed sleep apnea data. I can always change that later — the reverse would be harder.
