# About the project

Night by Night is the lab journal of an ongoing research journey. Not a tutorial portal, not a medical guide, not a commercial project.

What you find here are the personal experiences and data-driven insights of a single patient who systematically analyzes his CPAP therapy — with self-built software, AI assistance, and the conviction that your own data deserves to be taken seriously.

!!! warning "Medical disclaimer"
    All content describes personal experience. I am not a doctor. Always discuss therapy changes with your treating physician.

## Why "Night by Night"?

Because sleep apnea therapy can't be understood in a single night. Every night is a data point. The picture only emerges over weeks, months, years.
