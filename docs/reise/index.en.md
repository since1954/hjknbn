# My Journey

Sleep apnea has been part of my life since 2009. For fifteen years I was a passive patient — machine on, machine off, see the doctor once a year. Until I started collecting my own data.

These entries tell the story from passive patient to active data analyst. Chronological, but each entry stands on its own.

- **[How it all began](wie-alles-begann.md)** — Fifteen years of sleep apnea, one night in a sleep lab, and the question that changed everything.
- **[The first night with data](die-erste-nacht-mit-daten.md)** — July 14, 2024. A pulse oximeter, a CPAP machine, and a spreadsheet.
- **[The device switch](der-geraetewechsel.md)** — A new CPAP machine, an AHI dropping by a factor of 8 — and an independent sleep study showing why.
